# {{ company.name }} Security Policy

## Access Control

{{ company.name }} requires MFA for critical systems and limits privileged access to authorized team members.

## Data Protection

Customer data is encrypted in transit and at rest where supported by the underlying infrastructure.

## Vendor Management

Vendors are reviewed based on the data they process, their criticality, and their contractual security commitments.
